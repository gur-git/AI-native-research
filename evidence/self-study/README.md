# evidence/self-study/

The maintainer's own AI-native working practice, observed as evidence. This is the first
**evidence stream** opened to move the methodology's guidelines off their all-`proposed`
starting line (see [`../../methodology/guidelines.md`](../../methodology/guidelines.md), which
states outright that none of the 15 guidelines rest on systematic evidence yet).

## Why this stream first

Guideline promotion (`proposed → testing → adopted`) and most of the open questions
([`../../foundation/03-open-questions.md`](../../foundation/03-open-questions.md)) resolve through
the **same** mechanism: instrumented observation of real AI-assisted research work. The cheapest,
fastest-signal instance of that work already exists — the maintainer's own months of AI-native
practice across this and other repos. Several guidelines describe disciplines the maintainer
already runs de facto (external project state, direction logging), so a retrospective can extract
signal immediately, and a prospective instrumentation can start accruing the rest.

## The honest limitation

This is **n=1 self-observation**, and `foundation/01-ai-attributes.md` A13 (the perception gap)
plus the METR finding behind G7 say the researcher is a poor judge of their own AI-assisted
productivity. So this stream is `weak`-to-`moderate` evidence by construction. It is paired,
deliberately, with the external streams — the lab survey
([`../lab-surveys/ai-tool-use-2026/`](../lab-surveys/ai-tool-use-2026/)) and, later, interviews —
which is where the generalization questions (Q2, Q4, Q12) actually get answered. Self-study tells
us what *to* test externally; it does not settle anything on its own.

## Division of labor (important)

To keep this honest: an assistant may record what is **observable from artifacts** (was a practice
followed? what file proves it?). It must **not** fill in the **outcome judgments** — did the
practice help, did a skill develop or atrophy — those are the maintainer's, and inventing them
would be both fabrication and exactly the kind of replacement this repo exists to prevent. Audit
cells needing that judgment are marked **[maintainer]**.
